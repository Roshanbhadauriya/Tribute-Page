About Jiraiya

Jiraiya was a legendary ninja who was known as the "Toad Sage" and was one of the Sannin, a trio of powerful ninja who were trained by the Third Hokage. Jiraiya was also the sensei of the main protagonist, Naruto Uzumaki.

Achievements

  • Jiraiya wrote a bestselling series of adult novels known as the "Make-Out" series.
  • Jiraiya was able to summon giant toads to aid him in battle.
  • Jiraiya was able to master the Sage Mode, a powerful technique that enhances his physical abilities and chakra control.
  • Jiraiya was able to defeat several powerful enemies including the Akatsuki member Pain.
